LTL Shipping
Less-Than-Truckload (LTL) shipping doesn’t need to be complex. From obtaining low-cost LTL quotes to managing freight transport through delivery, our dedicated LTL support team is available 24/7 to assist your team with every shipment. As a valued GlobalTranz customer, you’ll gain access to our extensive local, regional, and national network of LTL carriers to capitalize on market competitive rates while maintaining the highest level of service.
Get More from Your LTL
Less than truckload shipping is used for shipments that are too large to be sent as parcel, but too small to fill an entire truckload. With GlobalTranz, you get more than just a way to book your LTL shipment—you benefit from our qualified carrier network, expert logistics support and leading technology.
Best-In-Class Carrier Network
Compare rates from our 120+ national, regional and local LTL carrier network without the hassle of managing each one individually. We vet all carriers for valid DOT number, MC authority, insurance, and safety ratings.
